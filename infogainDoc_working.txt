INFOGAIN INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED - FINAL REPORT

Infogain India Private Limited is a global IT and digital engineering
company delivering services in cloud, AI, data analytics, and enterprise
applications. The company focuses on helping enterprises modernize
legacy systems, improve customer experience, and drive innovation.

The organization operates with a global delivery model and has strong
engineering capabilities. Its India centers serve as major hubs for
development, testing, and innovation.

Services include digital engineering, cloud migration, AI/ML solutions,
cybersecurity, and enterprise systems like ERP and CRM. Infogain works
across industries such as travel, retail, insurance, and healthcare.

Employee reviews across platforms like Glassdoor and Indeed highlight a
balanced work culture, good learning opportunities, and flexible working
conditions. However, some concerns include slower salary growth and
manager-dependent experiences.

The hiring process typically includes aptitude tests, coding rounds,
technical interviews, and HR discussions. The company hires software
engineers, data engineers, cloud specialists, and AI professionals.

Infogain promotes diversity, inclusion, and ESG initiatives, ensuring
ethical business practices and employee well-being.

Competitors include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and Accenture. Compared to
them, Infogain is mid-sized but strong in niche digital engineering
capabilities.

Future growth areas include generative AI, automation, and cloud-native
development.
